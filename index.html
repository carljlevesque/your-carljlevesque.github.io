About

Carl Levesque is a career writer and editor, creating content on an array of topics that have always kept the work interesting: industry, public policy, leadership, the arts and sports. While his work includes interviews with actor James Earl Jones and former U.S. Secretary of Veterans Affairs and Army Chief of Staff Eric Shinseki, his claim to fame is writing features for a pair of golf magazines without ever having swung a club. He has an M.A. in writing with a concentration in fiction from Johns Hopkins University. He lives in Frederick, Maryland.
